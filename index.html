Kia ora! Welcome...

...to this personal project of mine. I entered the world of programming around 2017 when I started high school, and today I am working towards a Software Engineering degree and with some luck a career in this great field.

Somehow after all this time I've hardly touched HTML, CSS, or JavaScript. I was busy spending time with C#, Python, Java, and more recently C, and SQL. But web development is only growing more exceedingly important than it already is, and learning JavaScript has always been on my to-do list so here we are.

This site is a way for me to wrap my head around the basics of web development, and my current plan is to turn it into a portfolio of sorts. Thank you for visiting my site, and joining me on this wonderful journey.
